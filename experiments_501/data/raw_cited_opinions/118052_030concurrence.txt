<author id="b327-5">Justice Scalia,</author>
<p id="AAC">with whom Justice Thomas joins, concurring in the judgment.</p>
<p id="b327-6">In my view, the Double Jeopardy Clause prohibits successive prosecution, not successive punishment. See <em>Department of Revenue of Mont. </em>v. <em>Kurth Ranch, </em>511 U. S. 767, 798 (1994) (Scalia, J., dissenting). Civil forfeiture proceedings of the sort at issue here are not criminal prosecutions, even under the standard of <em>Kennedy </em>v. <em>Mendoza-Martinez, </em>372 U. S. 144, 164 (1963), and <em>United States </em>v. <em>Ward, </em>448 U. S. 242, 248-251 (1980).</p>
