<p id="b280-9">I join Parts I, II-A, II-C, and III of the Court’s opinion. Because the Court ultimately resolves this case under the <page-number citation-index="1" label="231">*231</page-number>Extradition Act, <span class="citation no-link">18 U. S. C. § 3182</span>, I do not find Part II-B, and its statements concerning the Extradition Clause of the Constitution, necessary to the decision of this case. Accordingly, I do not subscribe to that part of the Court’s opinion.
